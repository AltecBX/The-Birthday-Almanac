The Birthday Almanac — famous birthdays, zodiac signs, birthstones & birth flowers

The Birthday Almanac is an interactive museum of famous birthdays and the patterns behind history's most influential lives. Discover people born today, look up any birthday zodiac sign, find your monthly birthstone and birth flower, and explore historical figures by birthday — by birthday calendar, by zodiac sign and element, by field, by era and century, and through curated collections — from the greatest minds in science to music icons and legendary athletes.

Famous birthdays & people born today

Browse a complete birthday calendar of more than six hundred influential figures — scientists, artists, leaders, musicians, athletes, writers and explorers — and see exactly who shares each date. Open Discover to find the famous birthdays today, the people born this week, and surprising birthday connections across the centuries. Every profile lists the person's birth date, zodiac sign, birth year, era, country and the curated collections they belong to.
